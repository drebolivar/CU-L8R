## **_Description:_**

CU-L8R ("see you later" or "queue later") is a localized watchlist for households that have had many a
difficult movie night trying to figure out what to watch.

---

## **_Technologies Used_**

- _MERN_
  - Mongoose
  - Express
  - ReactJS
  - NodeJS
